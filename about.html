Our Mission

Our mission is to raise awareness about the digital divide and its impact on society. We believe that everyone should have equal access to technology and its benefits, regardless of their socioeconomic status.

Our Story

Bridging the Digital Divide was founded in 2023 by a group of passionate individuals who recognized the importance of digital literacy skills and access to technology. Since then, we have been working tirelessly to provide education and resources to those who need it most.
