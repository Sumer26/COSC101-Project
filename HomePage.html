About

Our mission is to raise awareness about the digital divide and its impact on society. We believe that everyone should have equal access to technology and its benefits, regardless of their socioeconomic status.

Issues

  • Lack of access to affordable internet
  • Limited availability of devices
  • Lack of digital literacy skills

Solutions

  • Government-funded programs to provide internet access to low-income households
  • Donations of used devices to be refurbished and distributed to those in need
  • Educational programs to teach digital literacy skills to underserved communities
